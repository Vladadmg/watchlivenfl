Atlanta Falcons v Green Bay Packers Live

After carving out their 3rd victory of the season, the Atlanta Falcons hope to remain on the winning track as they face off against the Green Bay Packers, this Sunday. The Falcons will be hosted by their opponents at the Lambeau Field. The Falcons managed to complete a much needed victory this past week, but they need to string more match winning performances, if they want to improve their overall record, this season. They have the potential to win, and will be keen to show that in the upcoming game.
