Denver Broncos v Dallas Cowboys Live

After completing their 4th emphatic wins in 4 games, the Denver Broncos look to further improve their record as they come up against the Dallas Cowboys, this Sunday. The Cowboys are scheduled to host their opponents at the AT&T Stadium. The Broncos will be a pretty confident team after their achievements in the first four games. In the last game, they humbled the Philadelphia Eagles by 52-20, and will be quite eager to complete yet another overwhelming win, and take their tally to 5 consecutive wins, this Sunday.

Peyton Manning led the way for the Broncos, in their last game. He has been quite brilliant with his passing in the last 4 games, and the team will largely depend on him, this Sunday. The Cowboys mixed performance continues, as they lost their last game, after securing victory in the 3rd game. They need to find consistency, as losing causes a break in momentum and confidence. They do have decent team and are expected to comeback hard, this Sunday. Tony Romo has been providing great service to his team, as he comes up with a great performance game after game. He would be disappointed, as he could not get his team over the line, this past week, but who knows, tomorrow might be his day.
