Kansas City Chiefs v Oakland Raiders Live

Click here to Watch Kansas City Chiefs v Oakland Raiders Live
                         
Sunday 15th December 20:05 - 23:05

 

After cruising their way to their 10th victory of the season, the Kansas City Chiefs look to build on further as they come up against the Oakland Raiders this Sunday. The Chiefs will be hosted by the Raiders at the O.co Colesiun for this week 15 encounter. The Chiefs had a lean patch of three games, but they were able to come up at their usual best, as they thrashed the Washington Redskins by 45-10, this past week. They will be hoping to carry the momentum forward and secure another victory under their name.

Alex Smith, as always, was the standout performer for the Chiefs in their last outing. He will be eager to continue with his consistent form and take the Chiefs to their 11th success of the season. The Raiders could not overcome the NY Jets, as they lost to them by 37-27 to register their 9th defeat of the season, this past week. They have had a few good performances as well, and they should take inspiration from them and come out confident in the next game. Matt McGloin did his job for the Raiders in the last game, but could not prevent a sorrow loss. He will be hoping to take his team to a much needed victory, this Sunday.
