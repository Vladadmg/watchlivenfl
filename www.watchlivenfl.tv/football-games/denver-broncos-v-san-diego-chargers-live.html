Denver Broncos v San Diego Chargers Live

Click here to Watch Denver Broncos v San Diego Chargers Live

Our Monday Night Football game this week is between the Denver Broncos and the San Diego Chargers and it starts at 8:30PM on October 15th, one of the Week Six games and taking place at Qualcomm Stadium. The Broncos have Peyton Manning as one of their secret weapons, and he has performed as expected when the Broncos (2-3) drafted him, but Denver has been having trouble holding onto the ball. Manning is hoping to help the Broncos advance further in the divisional race between these two AFC West rivals.

The San Diego Chargers are 3 and 2 right now and they do have one thing going for them in this game. Historically, Peyton Manning has not been very effective against the Chargers in the past few years, losing five of six starts, with just 11 touchdowns and 15 interceptions. In fact when it comes to interceptions, he threw six in the game on November 11th, 2007 and then in 2010, he threw four in a loss that ended 36 to 14. The Chargers do have Ryan Mathews who rushed for 80 yards last week and had a touchdown as well.
