After registering their 4th loss of the season, the Baltimore Ravens search for a spark of individual brilliance, as they lock horns against the Cleveland Browns, this Sunday. The Ravens mixed performance continues in the season, as they suffer from their 4th loss that came against the Pittsburgh Steelers by 19-16, this past week. They would be training hard and planning for their upcoming game, as they search for consistency in their performance.

Joe Flacco had a decent outing, but he will not be feeling satisfied due to his team’s loss, this past week. He will be hoping take his team to a much-needed victory this Sunday, and thus take their tally to 4 wins in the season. The Browns slip in form continued as they were overhauled by the Kansas City Chiefs by 23-17, past week. They were on course with two consecutive wins till the 5th week, but all has been worse for them after that. They will be desperate to get back on the winning track, this Sunday. Jason Campbell was the new performer for the Browns in the last game, and he will be feeling quite confident of repeating another similar performance, this Sunday.
