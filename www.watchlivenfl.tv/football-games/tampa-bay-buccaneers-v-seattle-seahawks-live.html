Tampa Bay Buccaneers v Seattle Seahawks Live

Click here to Watch Tampa Bay Buccaneers v Seattle Seahawks Live

After suffering from their 7th defeat of the season in 7 games, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers hope for less worse, as they team up for their 8th game against the Seattle Seahawks, this Sunday. The Seahawks will play host to the Buccaneers at the CenturyLink Field. The Buccaneers have been at a low point of their performance, and it looks pretty difficult for them to make a comeback into the season. They lost their last game to the Carolina Panthers by 31-13, and they will have to play above their potential if they want to challenge the Seahawks, this Sunday.

Mike Glennon, as always, stood for the Buccaneers, as the lone performer, this past week. If he gets enough support from other players, who knows the Buccaneers might pull off a surprise, this Sunday. The Seahawks completed their 7th victory of the season, this past week. They achieved the feat against the Saint Louis Rams by 14-9, and will be hoping to secure a more comfortable victory in the upcoming game. Russell Wilson displayed good passing skills, as he took his team to an important victory, past week. He will be hoping to further improve his performance, and complete yet another victory for his side, this Sunday.
