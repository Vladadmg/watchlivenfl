Buffalo Bills v Arizona Cardinals Live

Click here to Watch Buffalo Bills v Arizona Cardinals Live
                         
Sunday 14th October 16:05 - 19:05

 

On Sunday, October 14th, the University of Phoenix Stadium will see Arizona host the Buffalo Bills at 4:05PM Eastern Time in the sixth week of the regular season, and right now, the Arizona Cardinals might just have a chance to grab another win from the Buffalo Bills, who have not played a very good defense so far this season. The Cardinals started this season out quite strong, winning four in a row for a great start, their best in almost forty years. However, last Thursday, they had their first loss of the season against the St. Louis Rams, ending 17 to 3.

The Buffalo Bills have had some problems with their defense this season. They are currently 2 and 3 and they have allowed just over 1,200 yards this season in the losses to the New England Patriots and the San Francisco 49ers. This is the most yardage that anyone has allowed in a two game span since the year 1950 when the New York Yanks allowed 1,227 yards. Last Sunday, they lost to the SF Forty-Niners with a final of 45 to 3 and they gave up 621 yards in that game, the most ever given up in franchise history.
