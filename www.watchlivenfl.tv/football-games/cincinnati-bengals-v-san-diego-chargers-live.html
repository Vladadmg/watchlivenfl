Cincinnati Bengals v San Diego Chargers Live

Click here to Watch Cincinnati Bengals v San Diego Chargers Live

After completing their 7th victory of the season, the Cincinnati Bengals look to secure their 8th one, as they come up against the San Diego Chargers, this Sunday. The Chargers are scheduled to host the Bengals at the Qualcomm Stadium for this week 13 game. The Bengals have performed quite well this season, and their last win against the Cleveland Browns by 41-20 shows that. They have had 4 bad games as well, and they will be hoping that this Sunday turns up to be their yet another good day, taking their tally to 8 wins.

Andy Dalton was quite impressive in the last game, as he showed excellent passing skills to complete a thrashing victory for his side. The Bengals will be expecting another great performance from him in the upcoming game. The Chargers managed to secure their 5th win of the season, as they overcame the Kansas City Chiefs by 41-38 in a tight finish. That last victory against a strong team would have infused a lot of confidence into the ranks, and the Chargers will be eager to take the field and beat their next opponents, this Sunday. Philip Rivers did a great job for the Chargers in the last game. He remains a key player for the Chargers in the next game as well.
