Baltimore Ravens v Kansas City Chiefs Live

Click here to Watch Baltimore Ravens v Kansas City Chiefs Live

On Sunday, October 7th, the Baltimore Ravens will visit the Kansas City Chiefs at 1:00PM. This game happens at Arrowhead Stadium and will be in the fifth week of the regular season. The Baltimore Ravens have had some problems with the offense and there are concerns about how they will perform against Kansas City on Sunday. The Ravens were on a break, so they should come back full strength. They are currently 3-1 and last week they played against the Cleveland Browns on Thursday, beating them 23 to 16.

The Chiefs obviously have the home field advantage, but the last time that these two teams met, The Ravens were able to take that game with a 30 to 7 win. That was a wildcard game. Baltimore was able to pick off Matt Cassel three different times during that game. For the fifteen turnovers that the team has, seven of them were thanks to incerceptions of a pass by Cassel, and he has fumbled three times. Last Sunday, the team went up against the San Diego Chargers and lost to them 37 to 20.
