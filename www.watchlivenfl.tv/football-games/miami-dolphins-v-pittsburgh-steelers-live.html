Miami Dolphins v Pittsburgh Steelers Live

Click here to Watch Miami Dolphins v Pittsburgh Steelers Live
                         
Sunday 8th December 17:00 - 20:00

 

After cruising to victory for the 6th time this season, the Miami Dolphins aim to show more consistency as they come up against the Pittsburgh Steelers, this Sunday. The Dolphins will be hosted by the Steelers at the Heinz Field. The Dolphins continued with their good performance, as they thrashed the NY Jets by 23-3, this past week. Their overall record in the season is good, but they will be trying to make it better by securing yet another victory under their belt.

Ryan Tannehill did a fine job for the Dolphins, as he displayed his passing skills to full effect, this past week. He will be hoping to come up with another improved performance, as the Dolphins eye their 7th victory of the season, this Sunday. The Steelers completed their 7th loss of the season, as they were overpowered by the Baltimore Ravens by 22-20, this past Thursday. They have shown average performance this season, hence they need to raise their game as soon as possible, and this Sunday might happen to be their day. Ben Roethlisberger was quite remarkable for the Steelers in the last game. The Steelers will be depending on his performance in the upcoming game.
