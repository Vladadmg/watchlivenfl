Baltimore Ravens v Miami Dolphins Live

Click here to Watch Baltimore Ravens v Miami Dolphins Live
                         
Sunday 6th October 17:00 - 20:00

 

After suffering from their 2nd loss in the last game, the Baltimore Ravens look for a much needed victory as they face the Miami Dolphins, this Sunday. The Dolphins will play host to the Ravens at the Sun Life Stadium. The Ravens had a bad beginning to the season, but bounced back strongly by winning two games in a row. However, their last defeat would have had an adverse effect on their confidence, and they will be hoping to restore it back with a good performance on the field, this Sunday.

Joe Flacco has been the star performer for the Ravens for quite a long time now, and his passing skills are the most valuable for the Ravens. He will be their main player for the all important encounter, this Sunday. After a great beginning to the season, winning three games on the trot, the Dolphins finally succumbed to the New Orleans Saint by 38-17. They would hope that it is just a one off bad game, and not a change of fortunes for the team, as they look to get back on the winning track against the Ravens. Ryan Tannehill is expected to lead the way for the Dolphins, as he always does for his team.
