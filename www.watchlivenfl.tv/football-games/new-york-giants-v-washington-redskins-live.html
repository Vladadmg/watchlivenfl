New York Giants v Washington Redskins Live

Click here to Watch New York Giants v Washington Redskins Live
                         
Monday 2nd December 20:30 - 23:30

 

After being humbled for the 7th time, this season, the New York Giants look to come up with a much better performance, as they face off against the Washington Redskins, this Sunday. FedEx Field, home to the Redskins, will be hosting this game. The NY Giants had a poor beginning to the season, then they managed to pull it back a little, but yet again they have suffered a loss that came against the Dallas Cowboys, this past week. They need to address some serious issues and work up a solution, if they want to compete at least, this Sunday.

Eli Manning displayed outstanding passing skills, but could not take his side to a victory in the last game. He will be hoping to come up with an improved performance, this Sunday. The Redskins suffered a heavy defeat against the San Francisco 49ers by 27-6, this past week. The last loss was their 8th of the season, and they will be quite short on confidence going into the next game. However, they might well have a sniff against the weak NY Giants. Robert Griffin III was quite decent with his passing, but will be feeling disappointed on his team’s loss, this past week. He will be desperate to take the Redskins to a much needed victory, this Sunday.
