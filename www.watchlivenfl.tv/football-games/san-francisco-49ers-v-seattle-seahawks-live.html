San Francisco 49ers v Seattle Seahawks Live

Click here to Watch San Francisco 49ers v Seattle Seahawks Live
                         
Sunday 19th January 22:30 - 01:30

 

The San Francisco 49ers and the Seattle Seahawks meet for the third time this season, in the NFC Championship game on Sunday. During the regular season, both sides won their home match. In taking the 49ers to a third consecutive championship game, in his first three seasons as head coach, Jim Harbaugh not only became the first man to achieve this feat, but also steered the 49ers to a 15th championship game, which ties the Steelers for the most appearances since 1970. San Francisco will certainly go into the Seahawks game with momentum, having come through the divisional game in Carolina, without allowing a single point in the second half, as they scored 17 unanswered points on the way to their 23-10 success.

Despite having lost on their last two visits to Seattle, and with a combined score to the Seahawks in those matches of 71-16, the 49ers are confident of progressing to yet another Super bowl appearance. Seahawks confirmed their number 1 seeded status with their 23-15 win over New Orleans, bringing them to their second NFC Championship game, the last one being in 2005, when they won to advance to Super Bowl XL. The 49ers will have to be particularly vigilant of Seattle's Pro Bowl running back, Marshawn Lynch, who set a franchise post-season record in rushing for 140 yards against New Orleans, scoring two touchdowns along the way. His play-off game career, has seen him rush for five touchdowns in five matches, and post three 100-yard games, from those five appearances.
