Houston Texans v Detroit Lions Live

Click here to Watch Houston Texans v Detroit Lions Live
                         
Friday 23rd November 00:30 - 03:30

 

On Thursday, November 22nd, the Detroit Lions and the Houston Texans are going to be playing at Ford Field at 12:30PM for one of the three Thanksgiving Day games being played that day. Right now, the Houston Texans come in at a near perfect record of nine and one and the Detroit Lions are four and six. People are used to seeing the Lions lose on Thanksgiving Day, but the Texans have had a stellar year, and this season again the only thing that the Detroit Lions might be remembered for is being put in the ground by Houston.

The Texans are working on their fifth straight road game, and this is the first time that it has happened in the history of the franchise, keeping the Lions with no wins on Thanksgiving for what will be the ninth year in a row. The Lions have actually played twenty-three different opponents on Thanksgiving over the years, but this is the first time that they will face the Houston Texans on this holiday. They have only played two games with the Texans in the past anyway, each taking home one win from the encounter.
