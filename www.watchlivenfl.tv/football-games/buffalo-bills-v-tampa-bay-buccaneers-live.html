Buffalo Bills v Tampa Bay Buccaneers Live

After going down for the 8th time, this season, the Buffalo Bills look to come up with something better as they face off against the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, this Sunday. Raymond James Stadium, home to the Buccaneers, will play host to this game. The Bills have not been up to their standards, this season, and their last loss against the Atlanta Falcons by 34-31 shows that. They will have to devise a different strategy, and play as a unit, if they want to come with an improved performance, this Sunday.

EJ Manuel was the only performer for the Bills in the last game. He will be aiming to take his side to a much needed victory by coming up with a big performance, this Sunday. The Buccaneers went down yet again, for the 9th time to be exact, and put an end to a 3 match winning streak. They will still be feeling confident with their recent performances, and will be hoping to comeback with a bang, this Sunday. Mike Glennon was quite extraordinary with his passing skills, this past week. He will be eager to take his side to a comeback victory, in the upcoming game.
