Minnesota Vikings v Cincinnati Bengals Live

Click here to Watch Minnesota Vikings v Cincinnati Bengals Live
                         
Sunday 22nd December 17:00 - 20:00

 

After completing only their 4th victory of the season, the Minnesota Vikings aim to come up with another victory, as they team up against the Cincinnati Bengals, this Sunday. Paul Brown Stadium, home to the Bengals, is scheduled to play host to this game. The Vikings have been quite average in the season, but they will be feeling quite relieved after thrashing the Philadelphia Eagles by 48-30, this past week. They will be trying to come up with another improved performance when they face the Bengals, this Sunday.

Matt Cassel was the main performer for the Vikings in their thrashing of the Eagles in the last game. He will be hoping to continue with the same form in the upcoming game. The Bengals went down for the 5th time, this season, as they could not overcome the spirited Pittsburgh Steelers by a margin of 30-20, this past week. They will be aiming for a much needed comeback, as they have only two games left to play. Andy Dalton, as always, performed pretty well for the Bengals, but could not prevent a loss for his side in the last game. He will be hoping to take his side over the line in the next game.
