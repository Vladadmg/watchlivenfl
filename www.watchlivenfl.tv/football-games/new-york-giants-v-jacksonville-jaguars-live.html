New York Giants v Jacksonville Jaguars Live

Click here to Watch New York Giants v Jacksonville Jaguars Live
                         
Friday 10th August 19:30 - 22:30 GMT

 

Friday, August 10th, the Jacksonville Jaguars host the New York Giants at EverBank Field at 7:30PM. The New York Giants are still standing proud from their win at Super Bowl XLVI. The Jaguars will be under a new head coach, like some of the other teams this week. Jack Del Rio was fired in November last season, after spending a great deal of time with the team. Eli Manning is still hoping to lead his team to victory as they start this season out with this exhibition game, off to a good start if they upset the Superbowl champs.

The New York Giants still have many of the key players that helped them take home that Super Bowl win last year, including Head Coach Tom Coughlin and Eli Manning at the quarterback position. If they do lose some of these games, then you might be tempted to point the finger at wide receiver Mario Manningham and running back Brandon Jacobs, who left the team during the off season. However, the Giants do have Rueben Randle as a new wide receiver and they – and he – will hope to duplicate the results from Manningham.
