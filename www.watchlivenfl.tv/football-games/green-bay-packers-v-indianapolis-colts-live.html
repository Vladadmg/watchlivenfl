Green Bay Packers v Indianapolis Colts Live

Click here to Watch Green Bay Packers v Indianapolis Colts Live
                         
Sunday 7th October 13:00 - 16:00

 

On the 7th of October, Sunday, at 1:00PM, the Green Bay Packers take on the Indianapolis Colts at the Lucas Oil Stadium in Week Five. The last time that these two teams met was in 2008 and the Packers took that game, 34 to 14 on October 19th. The Colts didn't play last week, which is why they have a record of 1-2 right now. The Packers faced off against the Saints, winning their only game so far during the regular season ending 28 to 17. That gives Green Bay a record of 1-3 so far this season. This is the first time the Packers are visiting Lucas Oil.

The Packers offense is a little stronger than the Colts Offense, in both passing and rushing, but the Packers defense is severely lacking while the Indianapolis Colts have a very strong defense. The Colts Offensive Coordinator Bruce Arians will be coaching this week as Chuck Pagano is out with Leukemia. This is the first time in the twenty years that Arians has been in charge of a game. This is also the beginning of a three game set of road games for the Packers. The Colts have 3 different receivers at over a hundred yards.
