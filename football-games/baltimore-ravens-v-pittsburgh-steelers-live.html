After suffering for their third loss in the previous game, the Baltimore Ravens search for a much needed victory, as they come up against the Pittsburgh Steelers, this Sunday. Heinz Field will play host to this all important encounter. The Ravens have had a mix season so far completing only three wins, to go with the same number of losses as well. They lost their last game to the Packers in a close finish by 19-17, and would be hoping to get over the line, this Sunday.

Joe Flacco was the star performer for the Ravens in their last game, as he showed superb passing skills in a losing cause. The Ravens will be expecting an even better performance from him, in the upcoming game. The Steelers finally overcame the winning drought, as they managed to overpower the New York Jets by 19-6, in their last game. This last game would have infused loads of confidence into the unit, and Steelers would be confident of completing another victory, this Sunday. Ben Roethlisberger was the best player for the Steelers in their thrashing of the Jets. He holds the key for the Steelers in the upcoming game as well.

The Pittsburgh Steelers are hoping that they can make it to the top of the AFC North when they play the Ravens on Sunday night. The Steelers have won four games in a row which means that they are now within just one game of the Ravens who lead the North.
