Cleveland Browns v New York Jets Live

Click here to Watch Cleveland Browns v New York Jets Live
                         
Sunday 22nd December 17:00 - 20:00

 

After suffering for yet another defeat, their 10th of the season, the Cleveland Browns hope for a miraculous turnaround, as they lock horns against the New York Jets, this Sunday. The Jets will play host to the Browns at the MetLife Stadium. The Browns continued with their degrading performance, as they were thrashed by the Chicago Bears by 38-31, this past week. They will be extremely low on confidence, but it will be important for them to forget their past losses and play without fear in the upcoming game.

Jason Campbell continued with his good performance, but lack of support meant another big loss for the Browns in the last game. The other players need to respond well, if the Browns want to enforce a turnaround in the next game. The Jets lost their 8th game of the season, and that loss came against the Carolina Panthers by 30-20, this past week. They will have to improve their skills and strategy quickly, if they want to end their season on a high, as there are only two games left to play. Geno Smith did well within his limits, but could not take his side to a much needed win, this past week. He will be hoping to string a match winning performance in the upcoming game.
