Green Bay Packers v Houston Texans Live

Click here to Watch Green Bay Packers v Houston Texans Live
                         
Sunday 14th October 20:20 - 23:20

 

On Sunday, October 14th, the Green Bay Packers visit the Houston Texans at Reliant Stadium in our Sunday Night Prime Time game, which is in week six of the regular season and is scheduled to begin at 8:20PM Eastern. The Texans are one of the two teams that have not yet lost a game in the NFL right now, but this week might be the week that it happens. The Texans lost Brian Cushing to an injury that will put him out for the rest of the season, but still, they will hopefully be able to pull it together when they host the Packers.

The Packers are coming away from another loss last weekend. They are two and three right now, and they have been plagued by injuries this season, but that's only one of the problems that they have. They are currently two games behind Chicago and Minnesota in the race for the NFC North. Last weekend, they were 21-3 at halftime and it looked like they were going to be able to increase their average to over .500, but they weren't able to keep it up and the final score ended 30 to 27, giving the Packers their first loss after leading by 18 at halftime since 1957.
