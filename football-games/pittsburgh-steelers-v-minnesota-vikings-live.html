Pittsburgh Steelers v Minnesota Vikings Live

Click here to Watch Pittsburgh Steelers v Minnesota Vikings Live
                         
Sunday 29th September 17:00 - 20:00

 

After completing another sorrow defeat in their last game, the Pittsburgh Steelers look to come up with something better, as they meet the Minnesota Vikings, this Sunday. The Vikings will be hosting the Steelers, at the Wembley Stadium. The Steelers have had one of the worse starts to the season, and their last performance was also dismal, losing to the Chicago bears by 23-40. They will be hoping that somehow they manage to string a match winning performance, and continue to build on the achieved momentum.

Ben Roethlisberger did his very best, but his passing skills could not help the team’s cause. He would be hoping for more support from other players to come up with a victory for the Steelers, this Sunday. The Vikings have also lost their first three games of the season, but they would feel they were a bit unlucky, as their games were closely contested. They just need to find a way to get over the line, and all will be fine, this Sunday. Christian Ponder showed good passing skills in the last game, but he would be feeling gutted, as he could not take his team to a much needed victory. He will be looking to play a key role in Sunday’s encounter.
