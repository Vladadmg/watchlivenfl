New York Jets v Tennessee Titans Live

Click here to Watch New York Jets v Tennessee Titans Live
                         
Sunday 29th September 20:05 - 23:05

 

After recovering well from the loss in their 2nd game, the New York Jets will now lock horns with the Tennessee Titans, this Sunday. The Titans are scheduled to host the Jets at the LP Field. The Jets managed to come out strong in their 3rd game, as they were able to overcome the Buffalo Bills by 27-20. They had a good start to the season, but lost their way in the 2nd game. Nevertheless, they will be quite eager to build on the generated momentum, this Sunday.

Santonio Holmes, Bilal Powell and Geno Smith, all did a great job in their respective positions, and thus helped their team to secure a good win. They will be hoping to build on their efforts, this Sunday. The Titans were also able to make a strong comeback in their last game, as they defeated the San Diego Chargers by 20-17. They also lost their 2nd game of the season, their only loss, and so will be practicing hard to avoid defeat and continue on the winning track. Jake Locker and Nate Washington were the standout performers for the Titans in the last game. The Titans will be expecting another good performance for them, this Sunday.
