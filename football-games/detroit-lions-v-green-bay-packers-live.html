Detroit Lions v Green Bay Packers Live

Click here to Watch Detroit Lions v Green Bay Packers Live
                         
Sunday 6th October 17:00 - 20:00

 

After completing their 3rd victory of the season, the Detroit Lions aim for longer consistency as they meet against the Green Bay Packers, this Sunday. Lambeau Field, home to the Packers, has been chosen to host this game. The Lions completed a pretty strong performance in the last game, as they humbled the Chicago Bears by 40-32 in a high scoring game. They started the season on a high, and then had a one off bad game, but are again on a roll securing 2 back to back victories, and searching for their 3rd one, this Sunday.

Reggie Bush and Matthew Stafford did a great job for their team, as their great skills were on display, this past week. The Lions will be expecting more good performances from the two, to help their cause. The Packers have had a mixed season so far with one victory in three games. Last time, they lost to the Cincinnati Bengals by a narrow margin, and thus will be practicing hard to get over the line, this Sunday. The Packers have quite good players in their rank, and some of them showed great skills in the last game. If all the players manage to get their small bit done, the Packers might well be in with a great chance, this Sunday.
