Baltimore Ravens v Buffalo Bills Live

Click here to Watch Baltimore Ravens v Buffalo Bills Live
                         
Sunday 29th September 17:00 - 20:00

 

After securing their second consecutive win of the regular season, the Baltimore Ravens will now face the Buffalo Bills, as the tournament progresses into its 4th week, this Sunday. Ralph Wilson Stadium, home to the Bills, will play host to this all important encounter. The Ravens have recovered quite nicely after their first game, as two back to back wins show their upward progress. They floored the Houston Texans by 30-9 in the last game, and will be looking to repeat a similar performance in the next game.

Joe Flacco and company did a brilliant job for their team this past week, as they dominated the Texans. Flacco will be looking to build on his performance from the previous games, this Sunday. The Buffalo Bills lost their second game of the season against the New York Jets, this past week. The Bills have just won a single game out of three, but their performance on the field has been better than what the stats suggest, however, they will be looking to register more wins to rectify their stats as well. Erik James Manuel was good with his passing as usual, but lack of support from the rushing players meant the Bills could not complete the victory. The players will be looking to play more responsibly this Sunday.
