Kansas City Chiefs v Tampa Bay Buccaneers Live

Click here to Watch Kansas City Chiefs v Tampa Bay Buccaneers Live
                         
Sunday 14th October 13:00 - 16:00

 

The Raymond James Stadium is the venue of the game on Sunday, October 14th between the Kansas City Chiefs and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in Week Six. This game will start at 1:00PM and both the Chiefs and the Bucs have had a rough start this season. The Chiefs in particular are hoping to avoid a third loss in a row, and they have Brady Quinn who will be starting for the first time in three years. KC ended up last year with a 7 and 9 record, but that was mostly because in the final five games of the season, they won three.

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers haven't exactly had the best start this season either, but they are doing better than the Kansas City Chiefs. The Buccaneers started the season off with a win against the Carolina Panthers, ending 16 to 10, but have lost the three games that they have played after that, and they lost them by a total of 15 points for all of the games. That means that they have been performing well, but they need to tweak their performance a little if they want to turn any future losses by just a few points into wins.
