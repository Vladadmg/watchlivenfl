Philadelphia Eagles v Denver Broncos Live

Click here to Watch Philadelphia Eagles v Denver Broncos Live
                         
Sunday 29th September 20:25 - 23:25

 

After losing two back to back games in this season, the Philadelphia Eagles look to prevent a third consecutive defeat, as they meet the Denver Broncos, this Sunday. Sport Authority Field at Mile High, home to the Broncos, is scheduled to host this match. The Eagles have had a sharp dip in form after winning the first game of the season. They lost their last game to the Kansas City Chiefs, by a margin of 26-16, and hence need to come up with a strong performance to get back on the winning track, this Sunday.

LeSean McCoy and Michael Vick looked decent in the last game. They will be hoping to come up with a match winning performance, as the Eagles search for their 2nd win of the season. The Broncos have been one of the few top teams in the season, up till now. They have won all their three games comfortably, and will be looking to take their tally to four in the upcoming game, this Sunday. Peyton Manning was the star for the Broncos yet again, as they managed to secure a big victory in the last game. He is expected to come up with yet another great performance, this Sunday.
