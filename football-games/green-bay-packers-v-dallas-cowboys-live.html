The Green Bay Packers excellent season continued as they completed their 9th win of the season, and they will now lock horns against the Dallas Cowboys, this Sunday. AT&T Stadium, home to the Cowboys, is scheduled to host this week 15 game. The Packers had a superb last game, as they overcame the Atlanta Falcons by 22-21 in a close finish. They did not look like completing a victory in the last game but they managed well, and they will have to play an attacking game, if they are to trouble the Cowboys at their home, this Sunday.

The Cowboys have been performing well so far with 7 wins out of 13 games, under their belt.
