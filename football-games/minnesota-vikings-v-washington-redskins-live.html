Minnesota Vikings v Washington Redskins Live

Click here to Watch Minnesota Vikings v Washington Redskins Live
                         
Sunday 14th October 16:15 - 19:15

 

On Sunday, October 14th we see the Washington Redskins and the Minnesota Vikings face off in week six of the regular season, with the game at FedEx Field at 4:25PM Eastern Time. The Minnesota Vikings are visiting the Redskins, and are looking for a fourth win in a row, with Robert Griffin III possibly coming back to start this game after being out with a concussion last week. Hopefully, he will be able to step out and make something happen for the Washington Redskins as they are currently on an eight game skid at home.

The Minnesota Vikings will have Adrian Peterson coming back this week. Last year, he had a knee injury that put him out for the entire season, but he seems to have recovered. They will solidify the top spot in the NFC North if they can week this week against Washington. The Vikings are currently 4 and 1 and they have the season so far to give them confidence for this game. If they can win this game against the Washington Redskins they will take the lead from Chicago, so it should be an interesting game.
