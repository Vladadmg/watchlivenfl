Buffalo Bills v Jacksonville Jaguars Live

Click here to Watch Buffalo Bills v Jacksonville Jaguars Live
                         
Sunday 15th December 17:00 - 20:00

 

After going down yet again, the 9th time in 13 games to be exact, the Buffalo Bills hope for an individual spark of brilliance, as they team up against the Jacksonville Jaguars, this Sunday. The Bills will be hosting the Jaguars at the EverBank Field. The Bills have been pretty ordinary in the season, and this time they were even worse, thrashed by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers by 27-6, this past week. They have only two games to improve a bit, and they will be desperate to carve out a victory in these two encounters.

EJ Manuel was the only performer for the Bills in their thrashing from the Buccaneers, this past week. He will be hoping to take his team over the finish line in the next game. After rising further from the pit they were in, at the start of the season, the Jaguars will be feeling a bit more at ease. They defeated the Houston Texans by 27-20, this past week and will be hoping to end the season on a high, first winning their 2nd last encounter, this Sunday. Chad Henne was the top performer for the Jaguars in their last win. They will be having high hopes from him in the next game.
