On Thursday, October 4th, the Arizona Cardinals take on the St Louis Rams at the Edward Jones Dome at 8:30PM. This game will mark week five of the regular season. Last week, the Arizona cardinals scored another feather in their caps to make It a perfect season so far, with a win over the Miami Dolphins. The Cards are 4 and nothing, but their opponents last week are now 1 and 3 thanks to the game in the fourth week of the regular season. This is the first time in almost 40 years that the Cardinals have looked so good.

As for the St. Louis Rams, they are now 2 and 2 thanks to their own Week Four victory. The Rams beat the Seattle Seahawks last week with a final of 19 to 13. One of the stars in last week's game came in the form of rookie kicker Greg Zuerlein. He has not missed a field goal attempt so far this season and his kicks found purchase four times in last wee's game, with two of the kicks around 60 yards back. In fact, he set a record by kicking a field goal that was over 50 yards and another over 60 yards in the same game.
