Tennessee Titans v Seattle Seahawks Live

Click here to Watch Tennessee Titans v Seattle Seahawks Live
                         
Sunday 13th October 20:05 - 23:05

 

After suffering from their 2nd loss of the season, the Tennessee Titans search for an important victory, as they come up against the Seattle Seahawks, this Sunday. The Seahawks will be hosting the Titans at the CenturyLink Field. The Titans were looking great with two consecutive wins in their 3rd and 4th games, but lost all the momentum to the Kansas City Chiefs by 26-17, in their previous game. They will be hoping to rectify their mistakes from the previous game, and emerge victorious against the Seahawks, this Sunday.

Ryan Fitzpatrick did a decent job for his side, but could not effect a third consecutive win, in the last game. He is expected to be the trump card for the Titans in their Sunday clash. The Seahawks finally had their 4 match winning streak broken, and that was achieved by the Indianapolis Colts by 34-28. They will be a bit disappointed and motivated to get back on the winning track, and thus a pretty solid performance is expected from them, this Sunday. Russell Wilson did his job for the Seahawks, as he displayed excellent passing skills in the last game. He is expected to come up with a match winning performance, and take his team back on the winning track.

On the 11th of August, Saturday, at 10:00PM, the Tennessee Titans takes on the Seattle Seahawks at CenturyLink Field. It is tough to call this game, or even make any predictions, because nothing is set in stone, and that includes who will be playing. The Seahawks were able to grab quarterback Matt Flynn from Green Bay and he will probably be starting, but they also have Tarvaris Jackson and Russell Wilson. Seattle's receivers are led by Sidney Rice and Doug Baldwin and it will be interesting to see them in action this year.

The Titans have Matt Hasselbeck in the QB position again this year and they should be in a very good spot to make it pretty far in this season. They also last the first round pick from last year Jake Locker and rookie Nick Stephens. Chris Johnson will be present for the entire training camp this season so he should add to the action and Javon Ringer is a more experienced player. They also have Steve Hutchison on the offensive line which should help Tennessee's running game. For defense, they hae Kerion Wimbley from Oakland as well as Will Witherspoon and Colin McCarthy.
